About CiviqPulse

A youth-led community analytics initiative using real data to drive real change in Brampton, Ontario.

🎯
Mission
Use data analytics to identify community challenges in Brampton and provide evidence-based recommendations that improve public spaces and quality of life.
👁️
Vision
A youth-led initiative helping residents, community organizations, and local government make better decisions using data.
🏆
Long-term goal
Portfolio of projects: Park Cleanliness, Youth Employment, Food Accessibility, and Walkability Index.
📂
Open data
All datasets, reports, and dashboards published openly so anyone can verify, challenge, or build on the findings.
